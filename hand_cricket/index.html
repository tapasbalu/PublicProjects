📖 How to Play

1. The Basics

This is a digital version of Hand Cricket! Use your webcam to show 0-6 fingers. If the batsman and bowler show the same number, the batsman is OUT! Otherwise, the batsman scores the runs shown on their hand.

2. Player Roles

  • 🏏 Batsman (and WK): Master batters. Play all shots (0-6). As a bowler, they are weak (max 4 runs allowed).
  • 🎳 Bowler: Master bowlers. Can bowl special deliveries. As a batsman, they are weak (max 4 runs allowed).
  • ⚡ All-Rounder: The balanced players. Can play all shots (0-6) and bowl normally (0-6). (Max 2 per team)

3. Special Deliveries

True Bowlers can bowl dangerous special deliveries (max 1 per over).

  • 💥 Yorker (Red Warning): Deadly precision! You MUST play 0 or 1 to defend it. Any other number is OUT!
  • 🔥 Bouncer (Orange Warning): High pace! You MUST play an attacking 4 or 6. Any other number is OUT!

4. Match Flow

Build your ultimate 10-player IPL squad. You must include at least exactly 10 players. Win the toss, set a massive target, and defend it to win!
